Pitch Finder 6.0

25 February 2024

February 2024 updates:

  • Published the underlying dataset to GitHub. This is a major change for GAA Pitch Finder — I was afraid of sharing the data before but think it makes sense to share it with the masses!
  • Presented this work at the Q1 Dublin Data Meetup.
  • The original version of the map from 6 years ago was posted to Reddit, leading to a lot of debate, adding a few new pitches and some great publicity.
