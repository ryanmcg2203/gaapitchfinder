Pitch Finder 3.0

28 December 2020 · 1 min read

2020 end of year site updates:

  • The main map has been migrated to Tableau Public instead of Google Maps.
  • The map is adaptive based on laptop vs mobile.
  • Filtering capabilities for Clubs, Pitches, and Counties.
  • No Pitch filtering on Mobile due to screen size constraints.
  • Removed Twitter links due to difficulties in URL Actions going to both Google Maps and Twitter on Mobile in a single Tooltip.
  • May add Light mode based on feedback, if intuitive on mobile.

Other Pitch Finder work throughout 2020:

  • Tableau viz of the Worst GAA Pitch to get Injured at.
  • Tableau viz of the most Isolated GAA Pitch.
  • GAA Pitch Finder data used to aid with Government COVID response.

Still thinking about making an app but doing nothing about it — maybe in 2021.
