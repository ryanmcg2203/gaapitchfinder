Pitch Finder 5.0

13 August 2023

August 2023 updates:

  • Minimal development updates — have been working on migrating the website to GA4.
  • Created a map that was used as the front cover for Grassroots GAA by PJ Cunningham.

Most of the work has been providing the GAA Pitch data to third parties on an ad-hoc basis. This includes:

  • Safe Routes to School programme
  • MyReport.ie
  • Social Capital research with Tufts University in Boston, via Eoin McGuirk
  • PhD research at Trinity College, Dublin
  • The ESB, for their internal GIS mapping systems
