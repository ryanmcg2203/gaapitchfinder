Support GAA Pitch Finder

GAA Pitch Finder is a free map and open dataset of GAA pitches in Ireland and around the world. If it has helped you find a pitch, plan a journey, or use the data in your own work, a small donation helps keep it moving.

What donations support

  • Keeping the public map, static pages, and dataset available.
  • Checking coordinates, directions links, and newly reported pitch details.
  • Improving open data, analysis, and map features for the GAA community.
