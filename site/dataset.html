Open GAA Pitch Dataset

GAA Pitch Finder publishes an open GAA club and pitch locations dataset maintained by Ryan McGuinness. It contains nearly 2,000 GAA pitches worldwide, including club names, pitch names, coordinates, directions, elevation, rainfall data, and regional groupings.

The dataset powers the public GAA Pitch Finder map, club pages, county pages, and directions pages. It is available for research, education, community projects, and other useful GAA mapping work.

View the dataset on GitHub →

What's included

  • Club name and pitch name
  • Latitude and longitude (WGS84)
  • Province, county, country and region
  • Elevation (metres above sea level)
  • Annual rainfall and rain days
  • Google Maps directions link

Coverage

  • Ireland — all 32 counties
  • Great Britain
  • North America (USA and Canada)
  • Rest of Europe
  • Australasia
  • Asia
  • Middle East
  • South America
