Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

GAA Pitch Finder is a free map and open dataset for GAA pitches. This page explains what information may be collected when you use the website.

Analytics

GAA Pitch Finder uses Google Analytics to understand broad usage patterns, such as page views, referral sources, device types, and popular pages. This helps keep the site useful and improve the map and dataset.

Location Features

The "Near me" feature asks your browser for your location so the site can find nearby pitches. Your location is used in your browser for that search and is not stored by GAA Pitch Finder.

Map And Direction Services

The site uses Leaflet and OpenStreetMap map tiles to display maps. Direction links open Google Maps in a new tab or app. These third-party services may receive information such as your IP address, browser details, or the map/location request needed to provide their services.

Contact

If you email GAA Pitch Finder, your email address and message are used only to reply to you or follow up on your request.

Donations

Donation links open PayPal. Any payment information is handled by PayPal, not by GAA Pitch Finder.

Hosting

The site is hosted with GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard technical information needed to serve the website.

Your Rights

If you have questions about privacy or want to ask about any personal information connected with an email you sent, contact gaapitchfinder@gmail.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
